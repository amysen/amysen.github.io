Amy Pajak

Master's Student & Research Assistant · GRASP Lab, University of Pennsylvania

Hi! I'm Amy 👋 I'm a master's student in Robotics at the University of Pennsylvania, supported by the Thouron Award, a full merit-based scholarship for graduate study. I'm currently working as a Research Assistant in the GRASP Lab, and a member of the Lifelong Machine Learning Research Group led by Eric Eaton where my research centres on continual / lifelong learning and embodied AI - building agents that keep learning over time and can generalize across new tasks.

Previously I was a Machine Learning Engineer at Meta (WhatsApp) and a Research Engineer at Goldman Sachs. I hold a First-Class Honours BSc (4.0 GPA) in Artificial Intelligence and Computer Science from the University of Birmingham.

Research Interests

I work on machine learning for agents that act in the world and keep learning over time. My interests span continual / lifelong learning, reinforcement learning, and embodied AI - and I enjoy turning recent research into working systems on real and simulated robots.

  • Continual & Lifelong LearningLearning over time without catastrophic forgetting.
  • Reinforcement LearningAgents that learn to act from interaction and reward.
  • Embodied AI & RoboticsPerception, reasoning, and control on real and simulated robots.
  • Agent Memory & ReasoningHow agents store, retrieve, and reuse knowledge.
